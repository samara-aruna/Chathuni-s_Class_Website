About Me

Chathuni Hirushika

Physics Research Assistant & Researcher

My Journey

With over a decade of experience in physics education, I've dedicated my career to making complex physics concepts accessible and engaging for students of all levels. My teaching philosophy centers on hands-on learning, real-world applications, and fostering critical thinking skills.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe that physics should be taught through a combination of theoretical understanding and practical application. My approach includes:

  • Interactive demonstrations and experiments
  • Real-world problem-solving scenarios
  • Collaborative learning environments
  • Integration of technology in the classroom

Research Interests

My research focuses on:

  • Particle Physics

Professional Development

I regularly participate in:

  • Physics education conferences
  • Teacher training workshops
  • Curriculum development committees
  • Educational technology seminars
